1. Predicting Stroke Likelihood - Mobile Stroke Unit

What it does: Our Mobile Stroke Unit is a revolutionary app designed to swiftly respond when you suspect a stroke. By gathering crucial medical and personal information, it predicts the likelihood of a stroke. If the risk is high, it initiates an immediate dispatch of a specialized medical team equipped with cutting-edge technology.

Why it is important:

Rapid Emergency Response:In the event of a possible stroke, time becomes of the essence. Don't hesitate to call for an ambulance immediately if you or a loved one experiences any of the following symptoms: sudden numbness or weakness on one side of the face, arm, or leg, sudden confusion, difficulty speaking or understanding speech, sudden vision problems in one or both eyes, sudden difficulty walking, dizziness, loss of balance, or lack of coordination. Swift action could be crucial, so don't delay in seeking help by calling for an ambulance.

Assessment with Comprehensive Data: The app gathers vital medical and personal information, allowing for a holistic assessment of your stroke risk. This comprehensive approach enhances the accuracy of predictions and ensures tailored care.

Immediate Access to Medical Experts With the Mobile Stroke Unit, you are immediately connected to a team of stroke specialists who can make critical decisions on the spot, significantly improving your chances of a positive outcome.

Empowering Stroke Awareness Beyond emergency situations, our app raises awareness about stroke risk factors and the importance of early detection, fostering a proactive approach to stroke prevention and overall well-being.

2. Predicting Stroke Type and Location - CT Scan Analysis

What it does: Once our Mobile Stroke Unit identifies a high likelihood of stroke, the specialized medical team on board conducts CT scans of your brain. Our advanced app then analyzes the results to accurately predict the presence, type, and location of the stroke.

Why it is important:

Advanced CT Scan Analysis Through sophisticated AI algorithms, our app analyzes CT scan results with unmatched precision. This invaluable technology aids medical professionals in promptly diagnosing strokes and providing the most appropriate treatment plan.

Optimized Treatment Strategies: By predicting the type and location of the stroke, our app enables medical professionals to select the most effective treatment strategy for your specific condition, minimizing potential complications and maximizing recovery.
